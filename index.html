Personal Details | Introduction

Hey, so I am a 20 year old student currently studying Networking Technologies in the Technological University of Dublin (TUD). I'm currently in my second year of this course with my final year of this course set to be in 2021 along with the new Grangegorman campus.

I'm an avid learner with an interest in just about anything if I can find a use for it in my life after all why not try make life more interesting or simpler? I particularly like taking an calculated approach to life by keeping everything organised if possible as every little counts in my book. There's also just no reason to stress myself under the pressure of deadlines if I are able to meet them beforehand which is why I try complete work early and to a high degree and consistency. Usually I find myself managing group projects but I also do not mind doing work by myself.

Ideally after finishing this course I hope to be working in the industry as a "Network Engineer" but I also have the option of completing an additional year to achieve a Level 8 QQI degree. But that isn't to say I wont look for a career in another sector especially if it interests me.
